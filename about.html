Meet the Owners


Bloom is a fairly new boba tea shop in Newark, California, founded in 2025 by a Taiwanese couple, Mei and Mung Lin. Born and raised in Taichung — the birthplace of boba tea — Mei and Mung’s story began long before Bloom. After meeting in their hometown and building a life together, they moved to California in search of better opportunities and education for their daughter.

Once settled, they longed to share a piece of Taichung’s tea culture with their new community. They noticed that while milk tea shops were everywhere, many relied on artificial powders and excessive sugars — masking the true essence of premium tea. Determined to bring authenticity back to the cup, the Lins founded Bloom with a mission to serve high-quality drinks that highlight the natural fragrance of real tea leaves.

Through their dedication to fresh ingredients, craftsmanship, and genuine hospitality, Bloom has quickly become a local favorite — a place where every sip tells a story. Their growing community of customers continues to inspire them, shaping the very values that Bloom stands for today!

Our Values

Quality

As we have previously mentioned, we craft every drink with premium ingredients and fresh tea leaves to ensure an authentic, balanced flavor in every sip and we will always continue to do so!

Community

Bloom is more than a tea shop — it’s a space where neighbors, families, and friends come together to connect and share joy. We love listening to our customers feedback in order to create a better Bloom!

Sustainability

From our responsibly sourced teas to our eco-friendly cups and recycled packaging to straws made from biodegradable materials. We’re committed to brewing a greener future, one cup at a time.

Our Promise

At Bloom, we believe that every cup we serve is a reflection of our care, our craft, and our roots. That’s why we’ll never cut corners when it comes to quality — from the handpicked tea leaves we brew to the creamy textures and balanced sweetness that make each drink unique. Every ingredient we use is chosen with intention, ensuring that what you taste is always fresh, authentic, and true to the origins of Taiwan’s finest tea traditions.


We also know that great tea isn’t just about flavor — it’s about people. Your feedback helps us grow and improve with every pour. Whether it’s a suggestion, a compliment, or a concern, we listen closely and take every voice to heart. At Bloom, we promise to always uphold honesty, quality, and community — because every customer deserves a drink made with integrity and care.
