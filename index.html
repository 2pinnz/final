Why Bloom?

Founded by a Taiwanese couple from Taichung—the birthplace of boba—Bloom brings fresh, authentic tea to Newark. We brew with real tea leaves, not powders, for bold flavor, non-artificial sweeteners, and a true taste of Taiwan in every cup.


Our Specility Drinks

Bloom Milk Tea

Indulge in our Premium Milk Tea, a rich blend of slow-brewed black tea and velvety cream, perfectly balanced for a smooth, satisfying finish.

Matcha Creama

Experience the bold earthiness of ceremonial-grade matcha topped with a luscious, slightly salted crema for a modern twist on tradition.
